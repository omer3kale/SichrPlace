üß≠ End-to-End: From Search to Signed Lease

This compact, step-by-step flow shows the entire experience from finding a property to signing a lease. Use this as a quick reference or follow the expanded sections below for details.

1 Discover Listings

Search by city or address, apply filters (price, bedrooms, property type), then open listing details to view photos, amenities and availability.

2 Contact & Schedule

Use the "Contact" or "Schedule Viewing" button on a listing. Provide preferred dates and a short message. We coordinate professional viewings on your behalf.

3 View & Verify

Attend the viewing. Verify the apartment and landlord ID. After viewing, leave feedback and ask for additional photos or documents if needed.

4 Negotiate & Confirm

Agree on terms with the landlord. Use our messaging to confirm rent, move-in date, and any deposit requirements. Keep all communications in-platform for safety.

5 Payments (Pilot) & Receipt

If the listing uses platform-managed payments (pilot), follow the guided payment flow. Otherwise, arrange payment with the landlord and obtain a receipt. Never wire money without verification.

6 Sign Agreement & Move In

Sign the lease digitally or in person. Save a copy of the lease and receipts. Confirm move-in logistics with the landlord (keys, inventory checklist).

7 Aftercare & Support

Need help? Contact support through email or live chat. Report suspicious listings immediately.

    üö® Important Safety Guidelines

    Your safety is our top priority. Please follow these guidelines when using SichrPlace:

    1 Verify Listings
    • Always request to see the property in person before paying
    • Verify the landlord's identity and ownership documents
    • Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true
    • Check for proper business licenses if dealing with agencies
    2 Secure Communication
    • Use our platform's messaging system for initial communications
    • Never share personal financial information via chat
    • Meet in public places for initial discussions
    • Bring a friend when viewing properties
    3 Financial Safety
    • Never wire money or transfer funds upfront
    • Use secure payment methods with buyer protection
    • Get receipts for all payments made
    • Read and understand all contract terms
    4 Report Suspicious Activity

    If you encounter any suspicious listings, users, or activities, report them immediately to our support team.

    üîß Troubleshooting Common Issues

    1 Account Creation Problems

    Issue: "Network error. Please try again" message

    Solutions:

    • Check your internet connection
    • Clear your browser cache and cookies
    • Try a different browser or device
    • Contact support if the issue persists
    2 Language Switcher Issues

    Issue: Language dropdown disappears when clicked

    Solutions:

    • Refresh the page
    • Clear browser cache
    • Disable browser extensions temporarily
    • Try using keyboard navigation (Tab key)
    3 Page Not Found Errors

    Issue: Footer links return "page not found"

    Solutions:

    • Try accessing pages directly from the main navigation
    • Check if you're using the correct URL
    • Contact support to report broken links
    4 Mobile Display Issues

    Issue: Website doesn't display properly on mobile

    Solutions:

    • Rotate your device to landscape mode
    • Zoom out to see full content
    • Use the desktop version if mobile issues persist
    • Update your mobile browser
